# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Abusive, derogatory, or demeaning comments or actions
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling
- Disruptive behavior or deliberate distraction
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying expected behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue on GitHub or contacting the maintainers through the repository.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.
